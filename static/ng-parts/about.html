What is Plant Challenge?

PlantChallenge is a brand new service for learning plants. If you have at least a basic knowledge of plants and plant taxonomy, this is the right place to deepen your knowledge. PlantChallenge is for students of botany, gardeners and all plant lovers.

This is just a beginning. We are currently building a sophisticated and entertaining learning system. It is based on advanced artificial learning models by Adaptive Learning Research group from the Masaryk University, doing cutting edge research on e-learning technology. We are continuously improving both backend (guts) and frontend (skin) of the whole web.
